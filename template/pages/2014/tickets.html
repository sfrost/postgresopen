Tickets for Postgres Open 2014

Welcome to the Postgres Open 2014 Ticket and Speaker Registration section!

Our newly updated ticketing and registration interface works in tandem with your postgresql.org community credentials. If you do not have a login you will be asked to create one.

(Please note, your community account will not be shared or spammed in accordance with our policies).

If you’re ready to acquire tickets - click here!

Registration Type Fee Comments
Regular Session (not including tutorials) $550 Available to all
Tutorial $200 Cost is per tutorial session

A quick overview of how the ticketing process works:

  • Log in or create an account with postgresql.org
  • You will be redirected to the Postgres Open registration form page
  • Once you have submitted your information for registration and ticketing, you will see an invoicing page for payment
    Please continue through the process and pay the invoice immediately or your registration and ticket will not be confirmed.
  • Payment for conference tickets is hosted via our partner Adyen's web interface
    Adyen payment processing is based in Europe and may not be able to accept corporate American Express cards. If yours is refused, please try a different form of payment or contact us at program2014@postgresopen.org.. We apologize for any inconvenience.
  • Once your payment has been authorized, you will be redirected back to our Postgres Open site and a copy of the transaction receipt will be emailed to you as a pdf
