  • (214) 922-8000

Hotel Information

Sheraton Dallas is located in downtown Dallas, Texas, a short light rail ride from DFW.

Airport Information

Sheraton Dallas is 5 miles from Dallas Love Field (DAL) and 19 miles from Dallas/Ft Worth International (DFW). We recommend taking the DART orange line from either airport to the hotel.

Chicago Midway International Airport (MDW)

Airport Shuttle Service

Shuttle service is available through GO Airport Express. Reservations are not required from Midway to downtown. Reservations are required for return trips to the airport.

Shuttles depart from Midway airport every 15 minutes for downtown service to Chicago hotels.

Please visit airportexpress.com for more information or call 1 (888) 284-3826.

Train

From Midway Airport, take the CTA Orange Line direction towards the Loop to the Clark/Lake station. Walk east on Lake to Dearborn, turn left on Dearborn, cross the river and the Hotel Chicago Downtown entrance is on the east (right) side of Dearborn.

If you miss the Clark/Lake station, don't worry! Get off at State/Lake, walk west on Lake, turn right onto Dearborn, cross the river and the Hotel Chicago entrance is still on the east (right) side of Dearborn.

Since the train goes directly to Midway, this is the easiest and most foolproof transportation from the hotel to Midway.

For additional information on the Chicago Transit Authority & fare information, please visit transitchicago.com.

Chicago O'Hare International Airport (ORD)

Airport Shuttle Service

Shuttle service is available through GO Airport Express. Reservations are not required from O'Hare to downtown. Reservations are required for return trips to the airport.

Shuttles leave O'Hare airport for downtown Chicago every 10 to 15 minutes from terminals 1E, 3F, 2D and 5E. Shuttle service begins at 4:00 a.m. for downtown hotels.

Please visit airportexpress.com for more information or call 1 (888) 284-3826.
