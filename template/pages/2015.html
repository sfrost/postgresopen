Join us in Dallas!

Connecting business with database innovation.

PostgresOpen 2015 will be held on September 16-September 18th in Dallas, at the Sheraton Dallas. It will deliver three days packed with tutorials and presentations about PostgreSQL and related technologies, one day of extended trainings, and the usual hallway and social track!

We target entrepreneurs, technologies and decision-makers on the leading edge of data management, open source database innovation and disruption of the database industry. PostgresOpen will promote the business of PostgreSQL as well as its use and development

Postgres Community

The PostgreSQL Community organizes conferences with many technical and business talks by developers and key users. In addition to PostgresOpen, PostgreSQL Europe will be holding a conference in the Fall of 2015- 2015.pgconf.eu.
